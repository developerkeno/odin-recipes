Easy Beef Bulgogi

Recipe Description

At home, cook the bulgogi on a hot cast iron grill pan. All you need is a warm bowl of rice on the side for the most satisfying dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 asian pear, peeled and grated
  • 2 tbsps of mirin, rice wine, or dry white wine
  • 2 tbsps sugar
  • 3 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsps toasted sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 lbs ribeye, sliced 1/8 inch thick
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • cooked rice, to serve

Steps

  1. Make the marinade.
  2. Marinate the beef.
  3. Cook the bulgogi.
  4. Serve.
