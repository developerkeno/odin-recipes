Baked Salmon

Recipe Description

This simple whole side of salmon with butter, garlic, and fresh herbs is flavorful and foolproof, thanks to roasting it in a foil packet.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. whole salmon fillet, skinned
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 4 tbsp unsaltedd butter, melted and cooled
  • 4 cloves garlic, grated or finely minced
  • 1 1/2 tsp honey
  • 1 1/2 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp fresh herbs (basic, chives, or parsley) chopped

Steps

  1. Arrange th rack, preheat the oven, and prepare the baking sheet.
  2. Prepare the salmon.
  3. Season the salmon.
  4. Make the butter sauce and pour over salmon.
  5. Seal the salon with foil.
  6. Bake the salmon.
  7. Broil the salmon.
  8. Serve.
